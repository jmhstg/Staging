AI Applications

Smarter Technologies monitors support AI applications as the data stream is the core building block that informs the design of algorithms that power intelligence processes in machines.

The scale of information we harvest allows for AI system designers to model everything from local, regional, national consumption trends through to occupancy rates and usage; informing decisions on stranded assets, sustainability to how and when equipment is used on site. Ultimately the depth of data can be used to inform any data driven approach or machine learning programme in real time.

Data is currently being shared with leading environmental consultancy groups, civil engineering practices, institutional investors as well as government departments such as the MOD.
