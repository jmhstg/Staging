Utility Metering Solutions

Reporting and Monitoring

Increasing the visibility of consumption through utility metering on our IoT-connected dashboard will provide a clear understanding of where expenditure on resources is allocated. This allows you to accurately identify opportunities to save.

Smart Meters

Our Orion-based automatic meter readers are suitable for retrofitting to existing meters to provide better utilities management on site. Reduce energy costs with wireless, battery-powered smart metering solutions with automated peak usage warnings and peak power output management.

Sub-Metering Solutions

Our submetering systems provide real-time information consumption data on any given machine or site, allowing you to gain precise utilities usage information on an individual basis.

Providing Benefits Across the Board

Electricity Load Balancing

Commercial buildings can save on energy consumption and costs by automatically streamlining energy usage using our load-balancing hardware adapter.

Peak Power Output

Our peak power systems regulate the machinery startup process and manage your company's peak power usage in real time, allowing you to gain complete control and visibility of your electricity and high usage machines.

Achieve Carbon Targets

Smarter Technologies is equipped to deliver carbon-neutral energy solutions and green energy to your business. Making the change to a net zero carbon business starts with reducing emissions and energy use, as well as shifting to renewables.

Power Supply Management

With our real-time and fully-automated power management notifications, you can be alerted in the case of power outage or power surges.
